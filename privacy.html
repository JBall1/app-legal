Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: We respect your privacy. We don't collect personal data, we don't track you, and we don't sell anything to third parties.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app data (such as your progress and settings) is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

Any data you create within the app remains on your device. This includes:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Progress and usage statistics
  • Any content you create or save

This data is not accessible to us and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties.

Apple Services

If you download our app from the App Store, Apple may collect certain information as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. This is handled by Apple and is outside our control.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, this applies to users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy
